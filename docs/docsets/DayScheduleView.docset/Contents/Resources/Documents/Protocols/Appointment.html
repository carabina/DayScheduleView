Appointment

@objc
public protocol Appointment : AnyObject

The Appointment protocol defines the shape of an appointment or event that is displayed in the DayScheduleView view. The Appointment protocol exposes the information that the DayScheduleView view needs in order to present the appointment in the schedule.
